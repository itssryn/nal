Introduction

Realising importance of technology science in this era, Mr. Sameer poudel and Mr. William Riwaj Lammichgane established CCRC IT CLUB in 2068 BS

Since then, CCRC IT CLUB has been making it's best efforts to flourish and acknowledge students. CCRC IT CLUB is a very welcoming club for all students with different backgrounds , ideas , status and academy performance. We seek and sharpen talents of students interested in IT without any biases From building a small robot to online education portals for school , we are family of crazy technophile of CCRC
